A Little About Us
Our company name personifies what we stand for, translated it means  ' I Live Sports '. This new start up forms a bridge between advertising and and sporting events with a vision of to transform the life of a sportsman in India and around the world. Vivo Desportés serves as a local football community which gives the young players an opportunity to become aware of the latest events organised by us. Our focus is on football, where we give each football player a chance to validate their level of skill by our authentication process. The young sports professional will be exposed to the major decision makers in the football community.
 



Our objective is that we give them enough exposure that they are empowered to chose for themselves whether they want to pursue football as a career or not, thereby becoming a decision maker and taking on responsibility for their own lives. There are multiple advertising and marketing plans associated to events that we offer. We will therefore be working on multiple advertising platforms such as theatre advertising, newspaper printed advertisements, dynamic advertisements on webpages, Hologram advertising, Flyers, Posters and many more.
